## Obtaining the credentials

### 1. Credentials

You will receive login access to the Back Office: <https://cert.payin.monnetpayments.com/>

### 2. Log in

Once you log in, go to the section profile; there you will find the credentials (API Key and Signature Key) that you will use to communicate with our Sandbox environment.

[Payin Monnet](https://cert.payin.monnetpayments.com/pages/auth/login)

### 3. Keys

This information can be retrieved from the back office, the notification URL must be configured in the back office as well. Section Admin> Merchant Data.
